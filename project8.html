Daily Logo Challenge

50 Logos Across 50 Days

The Daily Logo Challenge is a design challenge specifically aimed at logo design. I signed up to this challenge as a way to better my skills as a designer. Once signed up, each day I would be sent an email with a new logo design challenge. This is my result and body of work from the full 50 day challenge. I believe this challenge has not only improved my design style, but also helped me practice my skills and improve my overall speed of working.
