50 Logos Across 50 Days

The Daily Logo Challenge is an intensive design challenge that is exclusively focused on logo design. I took on this challenge as a means of enhancing my skills as a designer, and to push myself creatively. During the challenge, I was tasked with creating a new logo design every day for 50 days. Each day, I received an email with a new prompt to inspire my work. The resulting body of work showcases my growth as a designer, as I experimented with a variety of styles and techniques to create distinctive and impactful logos. This challenge not only helped me refine my design style but also improved my ability to work under pressure and enhance my overall speed of working.
