About me

In life and the world around us, everything has been designed and created with a unique purpose and meaning and this has always fascinated me. Art is a way of life, which can provide creative opportunities to investigate and explore. I value these opportunities to develop my artistic and creative skills. I see art as an approach to thinking, with no limits or boundaries, an exploration of concepts and ideas as well as imagery.

I am a Southampton based graphic designer with a natural ability to identify and solve problems in innovative ways. I have always loved expressing my creativity. I hold a high graphical skill level and have approximately nine years of graphic design experience. I have a working knowledge of Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop and InDesign and use these tools to create iconic logos, advertisements, posters and other forms of design for a range of clients.
